upnp should be disabled on the router for best results

but for windows, you can disable the 
SSDP Discovery service
and
UPnP Device Host

so that in theory it should not respond to upnp probes, you can check with this test here

https://www.grc.com/intro.htm

to find the test go to their Services tab at the top and click Shields Up

after proceeding you should see an orange button for their instant exposure test
